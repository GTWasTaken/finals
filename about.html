Burgers Like No Other

Jollibee is one of the long standing fast-food chains in the Philippines. It has also spread its joy all over the world, from Asia to America, spanning over 1400 branches overall. Starting all the way back to the 70s, Jollibee transitioned from being originally a burger-only store to selling other items, primarily fried chicken. Since then it has opened more and more branches over the years to make it into what it is today.
